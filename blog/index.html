Where Now? You carrer choices
Feb/2020

I studied Engineering at University after which I went into the field in ​telecoms. This opportunity afforded me the chance to work in many ​different countries and see parts of the world I never thought I'd see. ​​

Along side this I spent my free time in outdoor pursuits, in particular sports climbing both indoor and out.​​ At some point something changed inside me and I felt that I need a​ change. I knew I loved working with technology but I want to explore possibilities that had been lingering in the back of my time. So I ​researched into starting up in aquaponics, solar panels, making gift items with a laser cutter and even considered becoming a carpenter.​​

Eventually it bought me back around to working with the internet. So ​after a period of self study and working on projects of my own choiceI became a Web Developer. I now work with Typescript and VueJs, helping ​to build a web app for the asset management industry.
